This game helps you test your knowledge on different logos. The game is simple. If you guess the right name of the logo shown, you get a point, if you don't, then you don't get a point. See, I told you it was simple.

You may click the show answer button to study or review the answers. If you get something wrong, you will get a message from the page. If you get something right, you will get a point without any message displayed. To begin, click the button below.
